Welcome to VeritasOne

Join us on our journey to redefine the future of healthcare—where your data, your decisions, and your health truly come first.

Empowering Patients Through Transparent Data Management

At VeritasOne, our vision is to empower patients by putting control of their healthcare data directly in their hands. We are on a mission to revolutionize how medical records are managed and shared.

Our free, unified EMR platform consolidates your medical data from every provider and hospital into one secure, patient‐centered repository that continually updates throughout your life.

Unlike other platforms, we empower you to take control of your health data, allowing you to monetize it on your own terms while contributing to groundbreaking medical research.

Most people don’t realize...

...that their identifiable health data is already being sold—without their knowledge or consent. Insurance companies sell billing data to pharma and marketing firms; Big Tech collects search/device data to infer conditions; wearable devices sell “anonymized” biometric data that can be re‐identified, and so on.

What Sets Us Apart

Strict Privacy Mode: Your data is for your eyes only. Share with doctors or family if you choose—never shared without permission.

De‐Identified Data for Health Research: Contribute to research anonymously while earning a share of the profits.

Full Data Sharing (Research + Ads): Choose to share all your data in exchange for additional monetization opportunities.
